Who Are We

Vlaby is the first virtual labs platform for school students in MENA, as it allows students and teachers to conduct laboratory experiments in an interactive environment that simulates a traditional science laboratory, and has all lab equipment and chemicals to conduct the experiment, also Vlaby is the first virtual labs platform in the world Apply the gamification system in virtual labs, where the student gets stars and prizes upon successfully passing the activities.

We look forward to the Flappy platform being the largest platform for virtual laboratories in Egypt and the Middle East, and to achieve leadership in providing an interactive educational service that ensures the sustainable development goals of providing high quality education to all students around the world.
